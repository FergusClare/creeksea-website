FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know about private AI infrastructure, our deployment process, and how creeksea.ai works.

Isn't private AI more expensive than using ChatGPT or cloud APIs?
Upfront, yes. But cloud AI pricing is per-token and scales with usage. Organizations running AI at volume often find that on-premises inference pays for itself within 6–12 months. More importantly, the cost of a compliance failure or data breach in a regulated industry vastly exceeds the cost of private infrastructure.
How does this compare to Azure OpenAI or AWS Bedrock?
Those services offer managed AI on cloud infrastructure, not on yours. Your data still travels to and is processed in someone else's data center. creeksea.ai deploys models on hardware you physically own and control. No third-party infrastructure involved, no API calls leaving your network.
What happens when the engagement ends?
Everything stays with you. The hardware, the models, the configurations, the integrations. There's no ongoing platform fee, no lock-in, and no dependency on creeksea.ai to keep things running. We can provide ongoing support retainers if you want them, but they're optional.
What models can you deploy?
We work with the leading open-weight models including Llama, Mistral, DeepSeek, Qwen, and others, selected based on your performance requirements, hardware constraints, and use case. We can also support fine-tuned models trained on your data.
How long does a typical deployment take?
Between 6 and 14 weeks depending on the tier. Foundations (Tier 1) deployments take around 6 weeks. Enterprise (Tier 4) deployments with multi-server architecture, SSO, and custom agents take closer to 14 weeks plus a 30-day hypercare period.
Do we need to buy our own hardware?
We can source and configure hardware for you, or work with equipment you already have. Our hardware page outlines recommended configurations from Mac Studios for small teams up to multi-GPU server clusters for enterprise workloads.
Is this HIPAA / FedRAMP / SOC 2 compliant?
Private AI deployed on your infrastructure inherits your existing compliance posture. We configure deployments with compliance-ready defaults, including encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access control, audit logging, and PII redaction. We work with your compliance team to meet your specific requirements. The final compliance determination is yours, but we build the technical foundation.
What if we outgrow our tier?
Every tier is designed to upgrade cleanly to the next. Your existing models, integrations, and configurations carry forward. We handle the migration, you don't start over.
