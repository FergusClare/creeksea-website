Pricing Note
What's not
in the fee.

The $18,000 setup fee covers professional services only — labor, configuration, integration work, agent development, documentation, and post-deploy support. It does not include hardware procurement costs for on-premises deployments, cloud GPU instance fees for cloud-hosted deployments, or any third-party SaaS subscriptions required by your chosen MCP integrations.

For on-premises deployments, hardware can be provided by your organization, sourced by Creeksea at cost, or financed over 24–36 months. For cloud-hosted deployments, we'll help you select and configure the right GPU cloud provider for your workload and budget. See our Hardware page for detailed configuration guidance.

All prices are starting rates in USD. Final pricing is determined after a scoping session and provided via a formal Statement of Work. Rates quoted in a signed SOW are locked for the duration of the engagement.
