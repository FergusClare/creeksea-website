Pricing Note
What's not
in the fee.

The $38,000 setup fee covers professional services only — labor, configuration, RAG pipeline build, agent development, SSO integration, documentation, staff training, and post-deploy support. It does not include hardware procurement costs for on-premises deployments, cloud compute or GPU instance fees, vector database hosting costs if cloud-based, or third-party API subscriptions required by connected MCP integrations.

For on-premises deployments, hardware can be provided by your organization, sourced by Creeksea at cost, or financed over 24–36 months. For hybrid configurations, we design the cloud component around your existing provider relationships. See our Hardware page for detailed configuration and cost guidance.

All prices are starting rates in USD. Final pricing is determined after a scoping session and provided via a formal Statement of Work. Rates quoted in a signed SOW are locked for the duration of the engagement.
