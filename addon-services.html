Pricing Note
How add-on
pricing works.

All prices shown are estimated starting ranges in USD. Final pricing for any add-on engagement is confirmed at kickoff after a brief scoping conversation to understand your environment, existing infrastructure, and specific requirements. Complex integrations — large document collections, multi-system orchestration, or compliance-heavy environments — will fall toward the higher end of the range.

Multi-add-on bundles of three or more services purchased simultaneously qualify for a 10% discount. Support retainer clients receive an additional discount on all add-on services (15% on Basic, 10% on Standard, 20% on Premium). Discounts are not combinable.

All add-on engagements include documentation and a handoff session. Rates quoted in a signed Statement of Work are locked for the duration of that engagement. Retainer agreements are month-to-month with no long-term commitment, or prepaid annually at a 10% discount.
