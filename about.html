About

The team behind your infrastructure.

creeksea.ai builds and manages private AI systems for organizations that can't afford to compromise on data control. We started because we watched enterprises hand their most sensitive data to cloud AI vendors, and saw the compliance gaps, vendor dependencies, and security risks that followed. We believe AI should run on hardware you own, in environments you control, with no third-party calls. That's all we do.

Leadership

The founding team.

01

Principles

Our approach.

03

You own everything.

Every creeksea.ai engagement results in AI infrastructure that you own outright, running on your hardware, behind your firewall, governed by your policies.

No dependencies.

When our engagement ends, everything stays with you. No lock-in, no recurring platform fees, no dependency on creeksea.ai to keep things running.

No cloud APIs.

We don't resell cloud APIs. We don't broker SaaS subscriptions. We build private AI systems that run entirely on hardware you control.
